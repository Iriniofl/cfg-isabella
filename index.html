About Me

Isabella Cortese has been one of the most iconic voices contemporary photography for more twenty years,with scores of magazine and book covers,over a dozen book and countless exhibitions around the world to her name. Cortese studied film at Boston University, before going on to work for a local newspaper. After several years of freelance work,Cortese made her first of what would become many trips to India. Traveling with little more than a bag of clothes and another of film,she made her way across the subcontinent,exploring countless beautiful countries with her camera.
